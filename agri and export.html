Agriculture in Sri Lanka

RICE PRODUCTION IN SRI LANKA

The processing of rice or paddy is one of the major Sri Lankan productions and staple foods. It cultivates throughout two Monsoon seasons in all districts of Sri Lanka. It's predicted that paddy uses approximately 708,000 hectares of land. The seasons are named Maha season and Yala season. The Maha Season begins in September and ends in the North-East Monsoon in March, and the Yala Season begins in May and ends in August.

TEA PLANTATIONS

Moisture, low temperatures and rainfall in the central highlands of the country offer a good climate which favors the cultivation of high-quality tea. Kandy and Nuwara Eliya in the Central Province, Badulla, Bandarawela and Haputale in the Uva Province, Galle, Matara and Mulkirigala in the South Province, and Ratnapura and Kegalle in the Sabaragamuwa Province are the main tea growing areas.

FRUITS AND VEGETABLES

Sri Lanka annually generates over 800,000 metric tons of fruits and vegetables and exports both new and refined varieties to many places around the world. Ninety percent of the fresh merchandise is targeted to the Middle East and the Maldives and approximately 75 percent of the refined goods are sold in Europe.

RUBBER & RUBBER BASED PRODUCTS

Sri Lanka has been one of the world's leading manufacturers of rubber, with a strong reputation for quality and durability. The rubber & rubber-based materials market is both environmentally friendly and sustainable with their natural rubber crops fending off the carbon pollution impact.

APPAREL & TEXTILES

Believed by many of the world's leading fashion brands, Sri Lanka produces and exports unguilty garments, that is, morally and sustainably sewn clothing. Our clothing and garment producers are also paying close attention to their workers and the community's wellbeing.

DIAMONDS, GEMS & JEWELLERY

70 percent of Sri Lanka's landmass can be gem-bearing. So, some of the most precious and valued gems of over 70 types, concealed underneath our soils and river beds. Apart from gemstones, Sri Lanka offers immaculate artistry in jewelry designing, carving & polishing of gems and diamonds.
